What is Levld?

Levld is what comes after the screen time app. Instead of just counting the hours you lose, it tracks your dopamine, the chemical that drives your focus and motivation, and shows you exactly how every app affects it. So you finally understand why and when you feel unmotivated, instead of guessing or blaming yourself.
